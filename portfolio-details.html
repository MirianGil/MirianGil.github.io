Services

As an economist specializing in data analysis and public policy, I offer my professional services to provide rigorous, evidence-based solutions to complex challenges in the education, health, and economic development sectors.

Data Analysis and Applied Statistics

Database cleaning, processing, and validation. Creation of algorithms for quantitative analysis and generation of indicators. Use of specialized software such as R, Stata, Python, and SQL for advanced analysis.

Economic Research

Preparation of research projects and writing of technical documents. Development of econometric studies and analysis of public policies. Management and analysis of national censuses and ENAHO, EEA and ENDES surveys.

Public Policy Consulting

Analysis and design of policies for economic and social development. Impact evaluation of government programs. Creation of reports and proposals for decision-making in sectors such as education and health.

Development of Economic Indicators and Models

Preparation of socioeconomic indicators for institutional reports. Construction of econometric models and analysis of trends in key sectors such as health, education and employment.

Management of Multidisciplinary Research Projects

Coordination of teams and resources for research projects on issues of poverty, inequality and productivity.

Training and Technical Advice

Training in the use of statistical tools and specialized software. Technical assistance for institutions seeking to implement monitoring and evaluation systems.
